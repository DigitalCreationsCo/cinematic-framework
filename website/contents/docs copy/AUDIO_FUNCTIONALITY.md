Cinematic-canvas can accept a user-provided audio track, turning your generative movie into a music video.

Audio input can be provided using the (Web Client)[], and (Cinematic-Canvas CLI)[]

Music videos by default, are the exact duration of the provided audio track.

Cinematic-canvas listens to your audio track, creates a blueprint of the music and lyrics, and uses it to create a music video that suits the music and your creative prompt.
